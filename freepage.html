Hello!

In this page I will tell you more about my hobbies

Reading Books

It started, as it so often does, with a novel. It was a fiction novel that offered a perfect escape from the awkwardness of being thirteen, or perhaps a gripping young adult story where the characters felt just as confused and passionate as I did.

I didn't just read the book; I fell into it. The bell would ring for dinner, and I'd finish the chapter under the table. I'd lose track of time, the pages blurring as the outside world dissolved into forests, castles, or busy high school hallways.

And from there grew the feeling of wanting to read more and more, whether it's fiction or non-fiction book.

Collecting Flower

There's something magical about collecting flowers. Each bloom tells a story, captured in a moment of perfect beauty before it fades away.

Whether pressed between the pages of a book or displayed in a vase, every flower becomes a treasured memory of a special day, a beautiful place, or a fleeting season.

This hobby has taught me to slow down and appreciate the small wonders that nature offers us every day.

Try Something New

Life is too short to stay in your comfort zone. Every new experience is an opportunity to discover something unexpected about yourself and the world around you.

From trying exotic cuisines to learning a new skill, each adventure adds color and richness to life's tapestry.

The thrill of stepping into the unknown has become one of my greatest joys, reminding me that growth happens at the edge of comfort.

Sight Seeing

Exploring new places opens your eyes to different perspectives and ways of living. Every destination has its own rhythm, its own story waiting to be discovered.

Whether it's wandering through historic streets, marveling at natural wonders, or simply watching the sunset from a new vantage point, each journey enriches the soul.
